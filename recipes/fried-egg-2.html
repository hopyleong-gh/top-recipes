Fried Eggs

Description

This the the best dish in the world. This is the easiest dish in the world.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pan
  2. Put some oil into the pan
  3. Break the egg into the pan
  4. Wait for the egg depending on how cooked you want
  5. Serve and enjoy
